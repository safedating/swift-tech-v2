Our Story

The story of Swift Tech began with a bad experience that I had with one of the identity verification service providers. In 2012, I was working as a developer of a financial institution. I was to integrate a famous KYC solution of that time as part of a major digitization project of a financial institute. After multiple failures and delays, the solution was implemented but we ended up cancelling the whole project and reverted to manual KYC. All because the codes of that KYC solution were inadequate and its tech staff was unable to provide us with sufficient support.


Established on the principles of authenticity, trust, and transparency, Swift Tech became the first company to offer free trials in the identity verification industry and kept its pricing public.

How to use your Swift Tech ID?
Once you get your Swift Tech digital ID, you can use it to prove to anyone you're meeting that your information has been verified. With your digital ID, you can be sure that all your meetups are secure.
So how can you verify someone? If they already have a Swift Tech ID, just ask for their unique QR code, and scan it. However, if they do not have an ID and you still choose to meet with them, Swift Tech takes no responsibility for any resulting risk or harm.
After scanning the QR code using your smartphone, you'll be directed to the Swift Tech website where you can view the verified certificate. This certificate verifies that all the necessary checks and protocols have been completed by Swift Tech and will confirm that they have been deemed trustworthy and reliable.
